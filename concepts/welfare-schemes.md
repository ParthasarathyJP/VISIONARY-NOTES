# 🌱 Welfare Schemes – TMK Vision 13

## 🌟 Introduction
TMK welfare schemes are designed to empower citizens with dignity, skills, and opportunity rather than dependency. Each initiative is rooted in the **Honey Bee Ethic** of collective responsibility and prosperity, ensuring welfare becomes a pathway to growth.

---

## 🛠️ Core Principles
1. **Dignity, Not Charity** – Welfare must reward effort, skills, and contribution.  
2. **Transparency** – Clear workflows, audit logs, and citizen dashboards.  
3. **Integration** – Welfare schemes link directly to education, industry, and agro‑business.  
4. **Collective Responsibility** – Failures are shared lessons, not individual blame.

---

## 🌍 Key Schemes

### Vocational Agro-Business Loan Scheme
- **Eligibility:** Vocational degree graduates in agro, logistics, recycling, renewable energy, MSME management.  
- **Benefit:** Low‑interest loans to start enterprises.  
- **Impact:** Transforms welfare into capital investment, rewarding skills with opportunity.

---

### Farmers Bank Prosperity Cycle
- Loan approval with soil & water validation  
- Crop insurance and regular inspections with audit logs  
- Yield purchase and Agro SEZ integration for value addition  
- Storage, logistics, and digital marketplace access  
- Profit‑sharing and green certification for exports  

---

### Human Value Card
- A national identity for education, prosperity, and inclusion.  
- Tracks citizen progress across welfare, education, and enterprise.  
- Ensures benefits are linked to dignity‑driven outcomes.

---

### Inclusive Schemes
- **Educational SEZ Medical College & Hospital** – With helicopter ambulance for emergency dignity care.  
- **AI Smart Towns** – Welfare integrated with digital infrastructure.  
- **Tourism Corridors** – Linking welfare with local enterprise and cultural prosperity.

# 🌾 Farmers Bank Prosperity Cycle – TMK Vision 13

## 🌟 Introduction
The **Farmers Bank Prosperity Cycle** is a dignity‑driven financial ecosystem designed to empower farmers with capital, security, and market access. It integrates loans, insurance, inspections, and exports into one transparent cycle, ensuring prosperity flows back to the community.

---

## 🔄 Cycle Stages

### 1. Loan Approval
- Soil and water validation before disbursement  
- Ensures sustainability and responsible credit allocation  

### 2. Insurance & Inspections
- Crop insurance for risk protection  
- Regular inspections with audit logs for transparency  

### 3. Yield Purchase & Value Addition
- Guaranteed procurement of harvest  
- Integration with **Agro SEZs** for processing and value addition  

### 4. Storage & Logistics
- Modern warehousing and cold storage facilities  
- Digital marketplace access for fair pricing  

### 5. Skill Development & Profit Sharing
- Training programs for modern agro‑business practices  
- Profit‑sharing models to reward farmers collectively  

### 6. Export & Certification
- Green certification for international markets  
- Linking farmers to global supply chains  

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **Transparency:** Audit logs and citizen dashboards  
- **Integration:** Loans, insurance, logistics, and exports in one cycle  
- **Resilience:** Risk protection through insurance and collective responsibility  
- **Prosperity:** Farmers rewarded with capital, skills, and global market access
